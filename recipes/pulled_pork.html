Slow Cooker Root Beer Pulled Pork

Mouthwateringly delicious sandwiches with very little work!

This simple slow cooker pulled pork recipe is big hit with my family of picky eaters. For a spicy kick, mix 1 part Frank's® RedHot® Buffalo sauce and 1 part of your favorite mustard-based BBQ sauce. You can use a pork loin instead of pork shoulder, if desired. Combine with your favorite BBQ sauce and enjoy.

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Cook: 8 hours:
  • Total: 8 hours 15 minutes
  • Servings: 10

Ingredients

Here is a list of all of the ingredients you will need for this recipe. Having everything ready to go will help with your prep and overall cooking experience!

  • 1 (4 pound) pork shoulder roast
  • 2 tablespoons coarse kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 (12 ounce) can or bottle of root beer
  • 1 (4 fluid ounce) bottle liquid smoke flavoring, or to taste
  • 4 cloves of garlic

Directions

  1. Place pork roast in a slow cooker.
  2. Rub with salt, douse it with root beer, liquid smoke, and garlic on top.
  3. Cook on low for 8 to 10 hours.
  4. Strain liquid through a fine mesh strainer, discard or save for another use.
  5. Add garlic cloves back to the pork.
  6. Shred the pork and then serve with your favorite BBQ sauce
