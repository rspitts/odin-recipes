Greek Sheet Pan Salmon with Asparagus

A simple, quick, healthy meal.

This is an amazing dish that you will love! The asparagus pairs extremely well with the salmon, while the Greek seasoning, feta cheese, and olives will transport you to the shores of Athens!

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes
  • Total: 25 minutes
  • Servings: 4 people

Ingredients

Here is a list of all of the ingredients you will need. Be sure to double check that you have everything ready to go to save time for prep and cooking!

  • 1.5 pounds salmon fillet
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 pound fresh, trimmed asparagus
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons Greek seasoning
  • 0.25 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Kalamata olives, pitted and finelly chopped
