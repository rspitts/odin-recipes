Grilled Balsamic Chicken Breast

Perfectly juicy and cripsy chicken in no time!

This is a simple and delicious recipe to make honey-balsamic glazed chicken breasts! It is the perfect item to add to any of your favorite sides or even a fresh salad.

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes
  • Additional: 1 hour 5 minutes
  • Total: 1 hour 25 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

Here is a list of all the ingredients needed for this recipe. It will help to cut down on prep time if you have everything ready to go!

  • 1 cup balsamic vinegear
  • 0.5 cup olive oil
  • 0.5 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
