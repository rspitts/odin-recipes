Grilled Balsamic Chicken Breast

Perfectly juicy and cripsy chicken in no time!

This is a simple and delicious recipe to make honey-balsamic glazed chicken breasts! It is the perfect item to add to any of your favorite sides or even a fresh salad.

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes
  • Additional: 1 hour 5 minutes
  • Total: 1 hour 25 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

Here is a list of all the ingredients needed for this recipe. It will help to cut down on prep time if you have everything ready to go!

  • 1 cup balsamic vinegear
  • 0.5 cup olive oil
  • 0.5 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Directions

  1. Whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, honey, dijon mustard, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  2. Combine chicken and 1/2 of the marinade in a resealable plastic bag and let it marinate for at least 1 hour, up to overnight. Save the rest of the marinade for grilling.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, to approximately 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly oil the grate.
  4. Place chicken on the grill and baste with the marinade. Lower grill to medium and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 6 to 8 minutes per side. Baste again when flipping.
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill when internal temperature reaches at least 165 degrees F. Let the meat rest for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
